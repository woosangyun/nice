VAN Service

We provide data transfer service between all off-line affiliates and card companies including network development, transfer of credit card approval data, purchase of card statement, and billing service.

01.Consumer -02.Off-line Affiliate -03.VAN -04.Credit card company / Bank

Service

  • Approval Service

    This is a payment approval/reject service after checking credit information of a customer if the customer buys a good from an affiliate where a card terminal or POS system is installed and requests approval of credit card payment.

  • Purchase Service

    This service is to transfer approval record and purchase data of affiliate where a payment is made using a credit card to a credit card company to transfer the money to the payment account of affiliate.

  • Cash Receipt

    If a consumer provides cash and card or telephone number, the affiliate issues a cash receipt using the card terminal POS system and transfers the cash payment record to the National Tax Service for income and tax deduction.

  • Cash IC Transaction Service

    A cash IC card for depositing and withdrawing money with CD.ATM (including credit and debit cards for cash deposition and withdrawal) can be used as a debit card at an affiliate having a card terminal or POS system.
