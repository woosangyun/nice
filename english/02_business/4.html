Mobile Service

Common Module for App Card

“Common module for app card” is a mobile payment service for customers to use the smartphone application (app) to register an app card (easy payment of Shihan, Samsung, KB, Hyundai, Lotte, and NH); select a payment card; and make payment using selected card.

Once a customer installs the common module for app card and register an app card (easy payment) with a brand app of an affiliate, Wallet, or mobile (online) store, he or she can make easy and off-line, online, or mobile credit (or debit) card payment just by entering a 6-digit password.
* It was developed according to app mobile card standard and approved according to the security certification (June 2014) by the Financial Supervisory Service
