  • Gift card image
    Gift Card
    A customer can buy (or charge) a real gift card and use it at designated affiliates to make payment and manage the balance.
  • Mobile gift card (MMS Type) image
    Mobile Gift Card (MMS Type)
    It is sent as an MMS to mobile devices, including smartphones, and can be used (or presented) immediately.
  • Smart Gift Gard (App Type) image
    Smart Gift Gard (App Type)
    A gift card can be purchased (or presented) anytime and anywhere using the app and can be used at designated affiliates with ease.
  • Product Exchange Coupon image
    Product Exchange Coupon
    Different mobile coupons of products can be purchased (or presented) in real time and then exchanged with the real products.
  • Paper Gift Card Coupon image
    Paper Gift Card Coupon
    A coupon can be received using a mobile device, including smartphones; certified at designated exchangers; and exchanged with a real one.
  • Paper Gift Card (Scratch Type) image
    Paper Gift Card (Scratch Type)
    When using a paper gift card at designated affiliates, its current state (used/unused, stolen, lost, etc.) can be checked to prevent illegal use of it.
  • Welfare Card image
    Welfare Card
    For the welfare of executives and employees, a card can be used at affiliates to make payments using annual points.
